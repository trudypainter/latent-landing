AI to accelerate understanding

Currently, Latent Lab jumpstarts the organization of all the MIT Media Lab projects using AI. It allows users to generate new ideas from existing projects and user uploaded ideas.

Latent Lab is now in the process of generalizing its approach to support internal knowledge organization at large enterprises. If you are interested in using Latent Lab with your organization, please contact dunnell@mit.edu.
